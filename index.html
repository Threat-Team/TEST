Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Minty.

How does Minty work?

Minty uses a combination of client-side and server-side checks to detect and prevent cheating in FiveM servers. Our system monitors for suspicious activity, unauthorized modifications, and known cheat signatures.

Will Minty affect server performance?

Minty is designed to have minimal impact on server performance. Our optimized code ensures that protection runs efficiently without causing lag or resource issues.

Is Minty compatible with all FiveM frameworks?

Yes, Minty is designed to be framework-agnostic and can be integrated with any FiveM framework including ESX, QBCore, and custom frameworks.

How often is Minty updated?

We provide regular updates to combat new cheating methods and improve detection capabilities. Updates are typically released every 2-4 weeks, with emergency patches as needed.

Do you offer support for installation and configuration?

Our team provides comprehensive support through our Discord server. We'll help you with installation, configuration, and troubleshooting.
